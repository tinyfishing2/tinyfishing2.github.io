About Tiny Fishing

Play Tiny Fishing, catch as many fish as possible, and explore the depth of the sea with various species available, from clownfish to dolphins, seahorses, and more. Play free online now and see how deep your hook can reach and whether you can catch the legendary tuna.

What is Tiny Fishing?

Tiny Fishing is a relaxing and addictive browser-based fishing game developed by Mad Buffer. In the game, you’ll cast your line, catch fish, and earn coins to upgrade your fishing gear to go deeper in the sea and catch bigger and rarer catches. 

Each fish has a cash value; rarer fish and ones living deeper in the sea are worth more money. Your in-game money earnings are always based on the rarity and number of fish you catch. Aim to earn the best coins possible and use them to upgrade your hook capacity and multiply your earning potential.

How to Play: Catch Fish, Upgrade, and Dive Deeper into the Ocean

Tiny Fishing is easy to play. Jump in, cast your line, and aim to earn a lot of money and go as deep as possible to catch higher-value sea creatures. Visit https://tinyfishing2.github.io/ to play this game online for free!

Catch the fish. Cast your line by clicking or tapping the screen; time it perfectly to reach maximum depth. Watch the meter closely and release at the right moment for the best result. Remember, the deeper the sea, the rarer and higher-tier the fish. Drag your mouse left and right to hook fish, targeting types of fish or sea creatures that generate the most value, especially the golden legendary ones.

Earn money and coins. The rarity and number of fish you catch at a time determine how much you can earn. At the beginning, you can get just a few, but as you play longer and invest in essential upgrades, you’ll earn more and more.

Buy upgrades. It’s a key to going deeper and earning more money in Tiny Fishing. With coins collected, you can upgrade Max Fishes, Max Depth, as well as Offline Earnings.

  • With Max Fishes increased, you can catch more fish at a time.
  • With Max Depth boosted, your hook can reach deeper levels of the ocean and catch higher-tier sea creatures.
  • With Offline Earnings upgrades, you can earn more idle income per minute.

Keep fishing and going as deep as you can. There’s no end to this game. Keep playing and see how far your hook can reach, and if you can catch the legendary tuna.

Game controls:

  • Click the left mouse button to cast the line.
  • Hold the left mouse button and drag it left and right to catch the fish and other sea species.

Tiny Fishing Key Game Features

  • Simple one-button controls and highly addictive loop gameplay
  • Different upgrade options to level up the hook capacity
  • Wide variety of sea species: fish, jellyfish, seahorse, clownfish, tuna, dolphin, sea urchin, etc.
  • Go deeper to explore the sea and catch rarer fish
  • No time limits; no stress or pressure

In short, Tiny Fishing offers an engaging and satisfying gameplay loop: catching, earning coins and in-game money, upgrading, and diving deeper into the sea. Play now & find out how deep you can cast the line.
